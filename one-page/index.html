About Us

In Short

At Rook & McDonald we deliver high-quality legal services specializing in Sports Law - specifically Hockey and Soccer. Founded in 2010, our firm has grown with a commitment to delivering efficient, detail-oriented and strategic guidance.

We serve as a key resource for top level managers, FA intermediaries, English Premier League players, leading European clubs, National Hockey League, Hong Kong Ice Hockey League, All-Japan Championship and International Ice Hockey League in relation to contentious and non-contentious issues, players' contracts, transfers and disciplinary matters. In short, we draft contracts, negotiate agreements, help resolve disputes and offer thoughtful legal advice. Our suggested solutions are tailored to your specific needs.

Not only does our team of attorneys know Sports Law, the founders both played professional Hockey and Soccer before moving on to Law School. We have a diverse and international clientele, with clients based in New York, Los Angeles, Toronto, London, Hong Kong, Europe and Australia.
